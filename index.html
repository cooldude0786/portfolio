Perspective on AI

Before AI, developing software required answering several questions: How do we solve the problem? Which language should we use? How do we implement it? What are the low-level and high-level designs (LLD and HLD)? And then, we had to write the code.

After AI, the process has become more streamlined: How do we solve the problem? Which language is best? What are the LLD and HLD?
